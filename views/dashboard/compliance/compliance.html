Means of Compliance
For each code clause, you should supply at least one Producer Statement from suitably qualified and experienced designers or building professionals involved in specific design. Calculations and drawings (where applicable) must accompany producer statements. Architectural drawings must be signed by the engineer verifying specific design details. Included with any Producer Statements should be any supporting material, including plans, design reports or documents required for compliance management.
